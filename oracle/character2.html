Meet Bud

Doug works for Digital MajWare as a Systems Architect and feels he is slowly dying on the job as he wanders aimlessly through the latest in a long line of server systems failures. His alter-ego is occupied with far more ‘thriller-like’ pursuits. Led by the tenets of Vodou, that say a dead person can be revived by a bokor (sorcerer), Yrkja Haggen was born. Since the Zombie Invasion of 2010, Yrkja's Bokor Rampage has moved through the underworld of basements and sewers to the relatively unclaimed territories of server rooms and cable rafters. Without a bokor his zombie astral will be forever craven.
